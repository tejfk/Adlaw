Our Story

Born Under the Cebu Sun

"Adlaw" means sun — or day — in Cebuano. It's the first thing you feel when morning comes: warmth on your skin, light through the window, the quiet promise that today can be good.

We built Adlaw Café around that feeling. Not just a place to grab coffee, but a space to pause. To breathe. To let the warmth of a well-made cup remind you that slowness is not laziness — it's intention.

Every blend we pour carries the soul of Cebu — from native Barako beans to tablea from the old markets of Carbon. This is coffee with memory.

"Hindi lang kape. Isa siyang ritwal — isang simula ng magandang araw."
